Warranty and care

Designed to stay clear, backed for 5 Years+.

How long before I can clean the glass?

Wait until the film fully cures. Your installer will confirm timing based on glass type, film series, and weather.

Can Polar film be installed on a windshield?

Yes, select clear and high-VLT series are designed for windshield use where local regulations allow it.

How do I register warranty?

Use the product code and dealer invoice after installation. Registered work receives 5 Years+ documented coverage.
